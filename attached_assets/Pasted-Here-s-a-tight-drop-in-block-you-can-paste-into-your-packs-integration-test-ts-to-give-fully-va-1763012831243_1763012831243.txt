Here’s a tight, drop-in block you can paste into your `packs.integration.test.ts` to give **fully valid minimal fixtures for all 10 new Packs**.

You can put this **right above** `const PACKS_UNDER_TEST` and then wire `makeMockPayload` from it.

And in your test body where you build `mockPayload`, you can tighten it to:

```ts
const payload = makeMockPayload ? makeMockPayload() : {};
const mockPayload = schema.parse(payload); // will throw if fixture is bad
mockedRunSkill.mockResolvedValueOnce(mockPayload);
```

Once that’s in place, Replit’s test runner exercising this file will give you a clean pass/fail signal for every Pack, end-to-end.
