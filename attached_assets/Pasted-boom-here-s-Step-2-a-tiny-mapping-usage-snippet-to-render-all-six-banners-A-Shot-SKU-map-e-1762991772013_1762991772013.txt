## C) How this “lights up”

* Before uploads: users see a soft placeholder per slot.
* After Nova uploads to S3 with the exact filenames:

  * `/fcc/lifestyle/{SHOT}_{SKU}_1920x800.webp` (and other sizes)
* The `<PictureBanner />` HEAD-checks `1920x800`; when it exists, the full sourceset renders.
* Alt text is pulled from `/api/seo/alt?key=/fcc/lifestyle/{SHOT}_{SKU}_1920x800.webp`.

## D) Quick checklist for the team (so it “just works”)

* Filenames match **exactly** the `baseKey` + `_{WIDTH}x{HEIGHT}.webp`.
* SEO CSV entries already point to the `1920x800` path with the real `{SKU}`.
* `alt_text.csv` has rows where `image_key` equals the **full filename** (e.g., `fcc/lifestyle/OL-1_Brunch_Banter_SKU-OL-PRIDE-001_1920x800.webp`).
* After uploads, revisit **Ops → Content Imports** only if SKUs changed (re-import SEO/alt).
