## Why this works

The connector endpoint was almost certainly receiving an empty `req.body` (no JSON parsing), so it forwarded `paths: []` → GigsterGarage correctly responded **paths[] required**.

Adding `express.json()` **at that route** fixes it even if global middleware order is weird.
