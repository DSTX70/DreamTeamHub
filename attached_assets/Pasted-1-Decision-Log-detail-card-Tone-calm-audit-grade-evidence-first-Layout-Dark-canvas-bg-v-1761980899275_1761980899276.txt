2) “Couldn’t reach this app” (error/empty state)

Tone: reassuring, actionable.

Layout

Full-bleed Synapse gradient background (bg-[var(--grad-synapse)]).

Centered glass panel with friendly illustration (ASCII mountain ok), concise copy, and primary/secondary actions.

Panel anatomy

Title: “Hmm… we couldn’t reach this app.”

Body: 1–2 lines explaining common causes.

Primary: Retry connection (solid); Secondary: Open in Replit (ghost) + Status/Docs links.

Optional: tiny diagnostic row (port, env, last ping) set in text-[var(--text-muted)].
