## Recording Tips

### Voice & Delivery

1. **Pace:** Speak at moderate pace - not too fast, not too slow
   - Aim for ~150-160 words per minute
   - Pause briefly between sections

2. **Tone Variation:**
   - Introduction: Upbeat, welcoming
   - Instruction: Professional, clear
   - Features: Enthusiastic but not over-the-top
   - Conclusion: Confident, inspiring

3. **Clarity:**
   - Enunciate clearly
   - Avoid filler words ("um", "uh", "like")
   - Stay hydrated during recording

4. **Energy:**
   - Maintain consistent energy throughout
   - Smile while speaking (it comes through in voice)
   - Stand while recording for better breath support

### Technical Quality

1. **Audio:**
   - Use quality microphone (USB condenser mic minimum)
   - Record in quiet room (close windows, turn off AC/fans)
   - Use pop filter to reduce plosives
   - Record separate audio track for easier editing

2. **Video:**
   - Full HD minimum (1920x1080)
   - 30fps or higher
   - Clear screen - no distracting elements
   - Cursor visible and smooth

3. **Lighting:** (if showing webcam)
   - Face well-lit, no harsh shadows
   - Soft light from front
   - Avoid backlighting

### Mistakes & Retakes

- **Don't worry about mistakes!**
  - Just pause, take a breath, and re-read the section
  - Edit out mistakes in post-production
- **Mark mistakes clearly:**
  - Clap hands or make loud noise
  - Easy to find in audio waveform for editing
- **Keep multiple takes:**
  - Record 2-3 versions of difficult sections
  - Choose best take in editing
